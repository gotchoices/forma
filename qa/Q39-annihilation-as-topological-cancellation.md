# Q39. Particle/antiparticle annihilation as topological cancellation

**Status:** Open — qualitative; testable prediction may exist
**Source:** claude-photon-knots-particle-structure.md
**Model era:** Model A

A positron may be the same (1,2) toroidal knot wound in the opposite sense. Annihilation = the two oppositely-wound knots unwinding each other, releasing the confined photon as free propagation. This reframes annihilation as topological cancellation rather than matter destruction. Is this consistent with energy/momentum conservation? Does it predict anything about the emission angles or polarizations of the annihilation photons?
