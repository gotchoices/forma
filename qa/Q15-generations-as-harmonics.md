# Q15. Generations as harmonics

**Status:** Answered
**Source:** S3-knot-zoo F2
**Model era:** Model A (WvM single-sheet)

m_μ/m_e ≈ 206.8 is not an integer. Does this rule out the simplest harmonic model?

Answered by R20 F17 — muon/tau are "hot electrons" (same (1,2) fundamental + uncharged sin-like harmonics adding mass), not simple overtones. Mass ratios aren't integers because the harmonic spectrum is thermal/free, not a single overtone. R21 F12 further constrains harmonics to sin-like (odd parity) modes.
