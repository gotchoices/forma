# Q69. DIS and scattering predictions from the harmonic proton

**Status:** Open — could constrain the harmonic spectrum
**Source:** Project assessment after R21
**Connects to:** R20, R21, Q26
**Model era:** Model B

R20's proton has internal structure (fundamental + uncharged harmonics). R21 Track 5 constrains harmonics to be sin-like. What does deep inelastic scattering look like in this model? Can we compute form factors or structure functions? This tests the model against hard experimental data without requiring quarks. The sin-like harmonics have specific spatial profiles on the curved torus — these determine the scattering response.
