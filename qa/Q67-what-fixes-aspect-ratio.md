# Q67. What fixes the aspect ratio r?

**Status:** Open — highest priority free-parameter problem
**Source:** R21 Track 5 conclusion, project-wide assessment
**Connects to:** Q52, R19, R21
**Model era:** Model B

The central free parameter. α(r, s) = 1/137 fixes s at each r, but nothing pins r. Potential second constraints: (a) energy extremality or stability; (b) matching a second observable (muon mass ratio, proton-electron mass ratio); (c) Q52's r = 1/2 from equal arc per winding; (d) neutrino mass-squared splittings (three near-degenerate mode triplet constraining r — see neutrino study if created). R21 Track 5 showed muon charge does NOT constrain r (parity selection rule instead).
