# Q36. Coulomb force as a subspace proximity effect

**Status:** Open — may be addressed by R13 results
**Source:** User question
**Model era:** Model A

We can model a point charge (E-field lines radiating from a point in 3D), but it is less clear why two separated charges exert a force on each other across empty 3D space. If both charges live as knots in a shared T³, the force might be a T³ geometric effect rather than a 3D field effect. Is the Coulomb potential the macro-space projection of knot-knot interaction energy in T³? How does this relate to what R13 is computing via KK decomposition?
