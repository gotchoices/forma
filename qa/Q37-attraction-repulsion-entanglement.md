# Q37. Are attraction/repulsion and entanglement the same phenomenon?

**Status:** Open — speculative but testable in principle
**Source:** User question
**Model era:** Model A

Conjecture: when two knots are in T³ proximity they interact — this manifests in 3D as the Coulomb force. When they separate, a residual topological link may persist — this manifests as entanglement. Same T³ mechanism, different regimes. If so, the force law and the entanglement statistics should share a common derivation. Is Coulomb force the "classical limit" of knot-knot interaction, and entanglement its topological residue?
