# Q40. Gravity as macro-space curvature sourced by T³ boundary conditions

**Status:** Open — Wheeler's program not yet translated into T³ language
**Source:** claude-photon-knots-particle-structure.md
**Model era:** Model A

Wheeler's geometrodynamics proposed particles as spacetime topology — "mass without mass, charge without charge." In our framework: each particle is a boundary between the material T³ and 3D macro-space; macro space curves around that boundary; that curvature is gravity. This is distinct from Q33 (which asks whether gravity and charge arise from the same photon — they don't). Here the question is specifically: is gravity the 3D curvature induced at the T³/3D interface, and can it be computed from the stress-energy of the confined photon?
