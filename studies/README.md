# Studies

Each subfolder contains a self-contained investigation with its own README, theory, and computational work.

## Active Studies

- `toroid-series/` — Can a series correction to the WvM charge estimate, based on nested toroidal sub-dimensions, converge to the measured electron charge?

## Future Study Ideas

- **Alternative field-distribution models for WvM charge.** The WvM charge estimate (q ≈ 0.91 e) relies on simplifying assumptions (homogeneous field, spherical cavity, specific charge radius). A study could explore whether more realistic field distributions close the 9% gap without requiring sub-structure.
