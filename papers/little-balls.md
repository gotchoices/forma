## Matter Path
- Material world is all we can sense, it is our reality
- Throughout history we've wanted to understand matter
- We big chunks of it, trees, rivers, oceans
- Time is mysterious, what is it
- We see little chunks of it: rocks, sand (little balls)
- It makes sense that as we break it down, we get smaller and smaller sand (littler balls)
- Why do they fall?  Eventual formulation (naming) of gravity
- Orbits discovered, gravity reinforced
- At some point, the atom was proposed (cite when, by whom), note etymology of atom, link to little indivisible ball
- The atom was proven (littlest ball)
- Crisis: the atom is not atomic, but the name sticks
- What do we discover?  More little balls
- What are they, how do they behave? (posit: electrons, protons, neutrons--more balls)
- Hey, orbits/ gravity here? No, so must be more forces, we name them
- Crisis: Those balls are not the smallest either
- Solution: more smaller balls: quarks, etc; Have to name more forces here too
- Crisis: Sometimes matter acts like a wave
- Parallel crisis: Not enough matter to explain galaxies: hypothesize dark matter

# Light (parallel path)
- Light comes from the sun, sun keeps us warm, God
- Light helps me see, keeps me safe, keeps me aware, light is good
- Light and darkness, spirituality, worship
- Light and eyes, key to knowledge
- Time is mortal, time vs eternity (time won't run out any more)

- Waves noticed, discovered, radio, EM, electricity

- Major unification: radio, light: all waves, tweener bands IR, UV, gamma, ...
- Crisis: sometimes light acts like matter (quantum -> photon)

## Resolution
- We've been too focused on little balls that sometimes (mysteriously) act like waves
- Or more accurately, waves that (mysteriously) act like little balls, just in time, just as we (the observer) needs them
- There is a better way to see the universe:
- Matter (the material world) is indeed little "balls" but they are made of light (i.e. energy), not more little balls.
- How is this possible?  More dimensions, matter is 6D, universe is 10D (hey, didn't QM say something about that)
- Doesn't explain light, so that's a given
- We're down to two things Light (energy) and Geometry
- What is geometry?  This is where God comes in, Geometry is design
- Design the dimensions, apply the energy, boom: creation
- All authentic pursuit of truth should end up in the same place
