# Papers

Authored documents presenting theories, results, and
reflections arising from the GRID+MaSt project.

These papers approach the same subject — the geometric
structure of matter and spacetime — from different
directions and for different audiences.  Not everyone
enters through the same door.

---

## Where to start

### The scientific path

If you think in equations, simulations, and testable
predictions, start here:

1. **[What If?](whatif.md)** — The method.  How dropping
   one assumption (matter is stuff) leads to a geometric
   model with one free parameter.  Accessible to any reader;
   no equations.

2. **[Matter from Light](matter-from-light.md)** — The
   particle model.  How confined photons on compact
   geometry produce mass, charge, spin, and the particle
   spectrum.  (Paper 1 of the technical series.)

3. **[The Ghost in the Machine](dark-matter.md)** — Dark
   matter as the uncharged majority of the same standing
   waves that produce visible particles.

For the lattice foundations beneath the particle model, see
the [primers](../primers/) (especially
[physics-from-fabric](../primers/physics-from-fabric.md) and
[alpha-in-grid](../primers/alpha-in-grid.md)) and the
[GRID technical documents](../grid/).

### The intuitive path

If you think in patterns, symbols, and complementary
structures, start here:

1. **[A Light Descent into Symbols](symbols.md)** — The
   duality.  Yin and yang, 1 and 0, E and B, delta and wye
   — the same complementary pattern at every scale, from
   the lattice to the constants.

2. **[Little Balls of Light](little-balls.md)** — The
   story.  From the Greeks to the Standard Model, humanity
   has been looking for smaller and smaller pieces of stuff.
   What if the pieces are light?

3. **[What If?](whatif.md)** — The method.  Both paths
   meet here.

### The spiritual path

If you are drawn to the intersection of geometry and
ancient wisdom:

1. **[137: The Divine Signature](signature.md)** — Is the
   fine-structure constant an artistic signature encoded in
   both physics and scripture?

2. **[Ancient Geometric Structure](ancients.md)** — The
   Genesis creation sequence and the GRID+MaSt coordinate
   hierarchy: structural correlations, honestly assessed.

3. **[A Light Descent into Symbols](symbols.md)** — The
   symbolic reading of the lattice geometry and the number
   137.036.

These papers are personal reflections, not scientific
claims.  They arise from the same project but do not
depend on it — and the science does not depend on them.
