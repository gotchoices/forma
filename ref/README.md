# Reference Material

This folder contains original reference material that is either not my own work or of a broad nature so as to be possibly applicable to multiple studies.

## Contents

- `WvM.pdf` — Williamson & van der Mark, "Is the electron a photon with toroidal topology?", *Annales de la Fondation Louis de Broglie*, Vol. 22 No. 2, 1997.
- `WvM-summary.md` — Living summary of the WvM paper: core claims, geometry, what it does and does not address, and corrections to our earlier misreadings. Updated as our understanding evolves.
- `dialog.md` — Formatted transcript of a Gemini conversation exploring tensors, the Compton wavelength, toroidal electron models, and a proposed "Harmonic Toroidal Fractal Theory."
- `dialog.txt` — Raw paste of the same conversation.
