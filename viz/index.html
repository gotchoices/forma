Interactive visualizations of the photon-knot electron model.
Click any card to open the tool. All tools run locally in the browser.

R3

Dual Torus — Flat & 3D

Side-by-side animation of a photon on a (1,2) geodesic: a 3D embedded torus on the left, the equivalent flat 2D rectangle on the right. Shows that the winding knot is a straight line on the flat material sheet — the physically correct picture for the material-dimension model.

dual-torus.html
S2

Torus Explorer

Interactive 3D torus with a/R slider. Morph continuously from a thin ring through the horn torus (a/R = 1) to a sphere-like blob. Preset buttons jump to notable values: Ring, Horn, the WvM charge-matching ratio (a/R ≈ 6.60), and the rotation horizon.

torus-explorer.html
R8

Multi-Winding Electron

Visualizes the compact torus geometry of the multi-winding electron model. Shows how many-orbit photon paths (winding number q ~ 100–200) fit a Compton-scale path length on a torus of radius ~ r_e. Explore the relationship between winding number, torus aspect ratio, and the self-consistent geometry.

multi-winding.html
GR

Geodesic Curvature

3D Cartesian geodesic grid around a spherical or toroidal Compton-zone boundary. Intrinsic spatial curvature (Schwarzschild / Brill-Lindquist) — no extrinsic "rubber sheet." Toggle geodesics vs field lines, sphere vs torus zone shape. 2D+Time mode shows worldlines tilting toward the mass. Adjustable curvature, density, a/R ratio, and opacity.

geodesic-curvature.html
R19

Shear Torus

Interactive torus with adjustable lattice shear. Ring lines twist into helices as shear increases, showing how the flat material-sheet lattice deforms. Visualises the (p,q) geodesic path under shear with effective winding q_eff = m − ns. Preset shear values include the α-matching s ≈ 0.157.

shear-torus.html
4D

Torus Slicer — 4D Cross-Sections

Sweeps a cutting plane through the 3D torus, mapping the sweep dimension to time. The cross-section (annulus) morphs from a single circle at the poles to a wide ring at the equator. Adjustable a/R, sweep period, and manual slice position. Toggle surface, wireframe, cutting plane, fill, and outlines independently.

torus-slice.html
T^d

Nested Torus — Recursive Wrapping

Visualise how periodic dimensions build on each other: a circle becomes a torus, a torus becomes a torus-of-a-torus, up to 5 levels of nesting. Each level wraps the previous structure around a new circle, producing concentric boundary tori. Per-level colour, opacity, and wireframe/shell controls let you peel back layers to see the nested structure inside.

nested-torus.html
R21

Torus Modes — Eigenmode Density

Standing-wave eigenmodes on the curved embedded torus. The curvature potential localises probability density at the outer equator, breaking the ±n₁ degeneracy into cos-like and sin-like standing waves. Density lobes glow as particle clouds inside a translucent torus shell. Multiple simultaneous modes with presets for the electron fundamental, R21 trio, and harmonic series. Adjustable ε (a/R) and shear s.

torus-modes.html
T³

T³ Slicer — 3D Slices of a 4D Torus

Visualises a T³ = T² × S¹ embedded in R⁴ by showing 3D hyperplane slices as the fourth coordinate sweeps. Two tori split and merge as the slice passes through the third periodic dimension. Tube-sweep mode varies the tube radius; Ring-sweep mode varies the major radius. Adjustable a/R, b/a (third-dimension coupling), sweep period, and manual θ₃ position. Small S¹ diagram shows the current slice on the θ₃ circle.
