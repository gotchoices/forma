GRID

Hex Bundles — Recursive Lattice

Visualizes the self-similar hexagonal clustering at the heart of the GRID hypothesis. Level 1 is a single hexagon; each higher level wraps 7 copies of the previous bundle into a larger cluster. Scale grows by √7 and the orientation rotates by 19.1° per level (the Gosper / flowsnake rep-tile construction). 7 distinct colors show which top-level sub-cluster each hex belongs to. Zoom, pan, and ← → keys to change level.

STUDIO

Torus Studio

Unified torus explorer combining 3D shape morphing, arbitrary (p,q) winding via spinners, lattice shear with primer-correct tube-line orientation, and an optional 2D flat-sheet panel — all synchronized to one animated photon. Supersedes the Torus Explorer and Dual Torus tools. Includes all physics readouts: outer edge, V/V ratio, q/e proxy, path length, slope, lattice angle, and qEff.

GR

Geodesic Curvature

3D Cartesian geodesic grid around a spherical or toroidal Compton-zone boundary. Intrinsic spatial curvature (Schwarzschild / Brill-Lindquist) — no extrinsic "rubber sheet." Toggle geodesics vs field lines, sphere vs torus zone shape. 2D+Time mode shows worldlines tilting toward the mass. Adjustable curvature, density, a/R ratio, and opacity.

geodesic-curvature.html
4D

Torus Slicer — 4D Cross-Sections

Sweeps a cutting plane through the 3D torus, mapping the sweep dimension to time. The cross-section (annulus) morphs from a single circle at the poles to a wide ring at the equator. Adjustable a/R, sweep period, and manual slice position. Toggle surface, wireframe, cutting plane, fill, and outlines independently.

torus-slice.html
T^d

Nested Torus — Compactified Dimensions

One way to visualize several compactified dimensions nested orthogonally in 3D. A circle sweeps into a torus, that torus sweeps into a torus-of-a-torus, and so on — each new ring threading through the previous level's hole. Up to 5 levels. This is not how dimensions are actually nested in MaSt; it is simply a pedagogical tool for building intuition about higher-dimensional periodic structures. Per-level colour, opacity, and wireframe/shell controls.

nested-torus.html
R21

Torus Modes — Eigenmode Density

Standing-wave eigenmodes on the curved embedded torus. The curvature potential localises probability density at the outer equator, breaking the ±n₁ degeneracy into cos-like and sin-like standing waves. Density lobes glow as particle clouds inside a translucent torus shell. Multiple simultaneous modes with presets for the electron fundamental, R21 trio, and harmonic series. Adjustable ε (a/R) and shear s.

torus-modes.html
Q95

Hydrogen Fusion — MaSt Model

Interactive proton-proton fusion physics. Left panel shows the nuclear potential (Coulomb + MaSt strong force) with adjustable well depth and range. Right panel plots the fusion rate vs temperature with Gamow tunneling and WKB barrier penetration. Sliders for temperature, B field, well depth, range. Scenario analysis for solar, tokamak, gas, and water electrolysis. Derives the solar core temperature from first principles.

fusion.html
T³

T³ Slicer — 3D Slices of a 4D Torus

Visualises a T³ = T² × S¹ embedded in R⁴ by showing 3D hyperplane slices as the fourth coordinate sweeps. Two tori split and merge as the slice passes through the third periodic dimension. Tube-sweep mode varies the tube radius; Ring-sweep mode varies the major radius. Adjustable a/R, b/a (third-dimension coupling), sweep period, and manual θ₃ position. Small S¹ diagram shows the current slice on the θ₃ circle.
